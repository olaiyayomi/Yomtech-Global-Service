About Yomtech

Yomtech Global Services is an organization focused on providing quality IT skills to Nigerians. The Institute was established to orientate the mind-set of people towards creativity, wealth creation, and to challenge them to be the best they can be by creating an enabling environment of friendliness and trust. Yomtech Global Services is accredited by Corporate Affairs Commission and Computer Professionals of Nigeria to offer professional Information Technology training to Nigerians.

What We Do

We offer Professional Information Technology Training at an affordable prices. Our training programs are flexible, you can choose any of our training sections that is convenient for you. Our training programs are practical intensive with qualified and competent instructors who will train you from their world of experience and expertise.
