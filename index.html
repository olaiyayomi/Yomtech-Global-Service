Our Training Programs

Yomtech Global Services provide affordable ICT/COMPUTER training programs to Nigerians. We offer great opportunities to connect with global clients and deal with challenging business processes. Experience a remarkable success and great job satisfaction with us.
Below are the various courses/programs we offer

Data Processing

Data Processing is a training program that teaches on the basic Microsoft Office Applications that are required in various disciplines. The programs is specifically designed for the BEGINNERS.

Graphics Design

Graphic Design is a training program that teaches on creating visual concepts, using computer software to communicate ideas that inspire, inform, and captivate consumers.

Web Design

Web design encompasses many different skills and disciplines in the production and maintenance of websites. The different areas of web design include web graphic design and interface design

We Development

Web development broadly refers to the tasks associated with developing websites and creating web applications for hosting via intranet or internet.

CCNA Routing and Switching

CCNA Routing and Switching validates the ability to install, configure, operate, and troubleshoot medium-size routed and switched networks.

Database Management

Database Management System (DBMS) is System Softwares for creating and managing databases with a systematic way to create, retrieve, update and manage data

Computer Maintenance & Repairs

Computer Repair is the process of identifying, troubleshooting and resolving problems and issues in a faulty computer.

Digital Marking

Digital marketing (also known as data-driven marketing) is the marketing of products or services using digital technologies, mainly on the Internet.

Computer Based Testing

Computer-based training (CBT) is any course of instruction whose primary means of delivery is a computer. This may be delivered via softwares/installed on a single computer.
This program is meant for JAMBITES and SSCE candidates
